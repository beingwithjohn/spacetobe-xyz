What a session looks like

      

        

          
~60 minutes

          
Zoom or in-person

          
Offered on a donation basisSpace is primarily offered over six-week flows, on a donation basis, after mutual discernment. This follows the spirit of dāna — the practice, ancient in Buddhist tradition, of offering the work freely and letting it be supported through generosity. Giving in this way is itself a practice of honesty, generosity, and skilfully looking after oneself. For a sense of scale, most people offer between $900 and $1,500 per six-week flow, and $150–250 per ongoing session.
